I'm Waqar Ahmad, a Video Editor and Motion Designer from Faisalabad, Pakistan — with 3+ years of hands-on experience turning raw footage into cinematic stories that move people.

My craft spans video editing, color grading, motion graphics, 3D animation, and visual effects. I specialize in YouTube content, commercial edits, social media reels, and anything that deserves to look world-class.
